# FinVoice Pilot Deployment Plan

## Phase 1: Internal Testing (Week 1-2)
- **Participants**: 10 bank employees
- **Goals**: Test core functionality, identify bugs
- **Metrics**: 
  - Success rate > 90%
  - Average response time < 3s
  - User satisfaction > 4/5

## Phase 2: Limited Beta (Week 3-4)
- **Participants**: 100 customers (diverse demographics)
- **Features**: Balance, Transfer, History
- **Support**: 24/7 helpdesk
- **Metrics**:
  - Daily active users > 60%
  - Transaction success rate > 95%
  - Customer support tickets < 10/day

## Phase 3: Expanded Beta (Month 2)
- **Participants**: 1,000 customers
- **New Features**: Loans, Bill payments, Reminders
- **Metrics**:
  - Cost savings vs call center
  - Customer retention
  - NPS score

## Phase 4: Full Rollout (Month 3+)
- **Target**: All customers
- **Infrastructure**: Auto-scaling, load balancing
- **Success Criteria**:
  - 50% adoption within 6 months
  - ROI positive within 12 months
  - NPS > 60

## Risk Mitigation
1. **Security**: Regular penetration testing
2. **Performance**: Load testing before each phase
3. **Compliance**: Legal review at each phase
4. **Rollback**: Instant rollback capability
